POETRY :: Sansein

Teri bahon se nikal aai hoon aaj main ,
Tujhe chod aai hoon aaj main ,
Bhut mushkilein aai tujhse dur jaane ke safar mein ,
Magar itni bhi nhi jitni aai thi tere paas aane ke safar mein ,
Tere sath dum ghutne sa laga hai ,
Tera mera sath chutne sa laga hai ,
Itna asan nhi tha tujhse dur chale aana ,
Magar asan raste kabhi chune hi nhi maine ,
Farq dikhne laga hai teri meri soch mein ,
Sawal uthne lage hain tere bare mein mere man mein ,
Un sawlon ke jawab nhi hain mere pass ,
Kya fayda ab jawab dhundke auur kkya hi fayda tera hisab rakh ke ,
Khushi toh is baat ki hai kii,
Dum nhi ghut ta ab mera tere sath mein ,
Kyunki teri bahon se nikal aai hoon aaj main ...........

POETRY :: Bacchpan

Jaha khushiyon ka dera tha.
Hasta gata ek muskurata sa chehra tha.
Hawa mein lehrata aur barish ki boondon mein bhig jata ek chota sa man tha.
Jise daant bhi chahe mar bhi do lekin battisiya dikhane se nhi chukta tha.
Gum gayi hai woh muskurahat kahin ,
Khoya hai masum sa baccha , shayad roya hai masoom sa baccha,
Ankhein timtimati thi , un timtimati aankhon mein ek taara sa chamakta tha ,
Ab unhi aankhon mein barish ki boondein jhalakti hai ,
Ruthne par maa manati , Apne hathon se khana khilati , lori gake usko sulati,
Ek muskurahat par sab kuch ho jata tha ,
Woh bacchpan , akhir woh bacchpan bhi kitna pyara tha......

POETRY :: Ahsas

Ek farz toh tera bhi tha mujhe lekar ,
Main khadhi thi jahan , wahan toh andhera bhi tha woh bhi diya lekar,
Sir jhuka hua mera bhi tha bawjood nazrein milakar,
Waqt ruk gaya mera bhi tha , jab tu gaya mujhe choo kar,
Tere samne main hara bhi tha , par nhi padhhta tujhe dekh kar ,
Sach samne tera bhi tha , magar nazar andaz kiya maine aankhein band kar,
Samajh ne toh waha maat khayi --
Jab maine dimag ki na suni dil ki sun kar,
Aur tumne koi aur chuni mujhe chod kar,
Man ki saaf thi , kismat ki haar thi,
Tumhe reham na aai , mujhe sharam na aayi ,
Har baar tum mujhe chodhde aur mujhse muh bhi na modhte ,
Kya hi falsafa samjha maine tumhe padhkar ,
Kaisi rivayat chali aa rhi tab se jab aayi pehli baar tumse mil kar ,
Aur rooh ne bhi waha maat khayi --
Jaha mujhe aitbaar hua ki tumhee ahsas nhi kii ----
Tmhara bhi ek farz tha mujhe lekar.......


Writing Journey

I (Shiphali Chauraisa) discovered my love for poetry back when I was in 10th grade. Writing became my way of exploring feelings and experiences that I had never truly lived but could vividly imagine.

Each poem I wrote was like stepping into a world I created, where my thoughts and emotions found a voice. As I continued my journey through a diploma in electronic engineering, poetry remained my refuge, a place where I could pour out the things I couldn't say out loud.

But then, life threw a curveball. When I couldn't get into any government engineering colleges, it felt like a door had slammed shut on my dreams. That disappointment weighed heavily on me, and in the midst of it all, I stopped writing.

My pen, which had once been my closest companion, fell silent. Yet, deep down, I knew that my love for poetry hadn't disappeared—it was just waiting for the right moment to resurface.

In the beginning, I was too scared to share my poems with anyone. The fear of being judged or misunderstood kept me from putting my work out there. But over time, I gathered the courage to post my poems on my private Instagram handle.

Even though my account was private and the likes were few, the recognition I received from those who did see my work meant the world to me. It felt like a small but significant step toward embracing my passion.

Though I've stepped away from writing for now, I can feel that this isn't the end of my journey. The words and emotions that once flowed so freely still linger within me, waiting patiently for the right moment to emerge.

I may have put down my pen for a while, but the desire to write is still there, alive and strong. I know that when the time is right, I'll return to my poetry, and my journey will continue, perhaps with even greater depth and passion.
